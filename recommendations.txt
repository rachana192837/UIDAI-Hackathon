STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS FOR UIDAI
================================================================================

1. Deploy Mobile Enrollment Units in High-Migration States [HIGH PRIORITY]
   Focus on Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh which account for majority of migrations
   Expected Impact: Ensure migrant populations maintain updated Aadhaar records

2. Seasonal Resource Allocation [MEDIUM PRIORITY]
   Increase staffing and centers during peak months (Jan)
   Expected Impact: Reduce wait times and improve service efficiency

3. Digital Literacy Programs [MEDIUM PRIORITY]
   Launch awareness campaigns for mobile number updates, especially in rural areas
   Expected Impact: Improve digital inclusion and service communication

4. Proactive Capacity Planning [MEDIUM PRIORITY]
   Prepare for predicted high-demand periods: January 2026, February 2026, March 2026
   Expected Impact: Prevent service bottlenecks

5. Children Biometric Update Campaigns [MEDIUM PRIORITY]
   Targeted drives for 5-year-olds and 15-year-olds transitioning
   Expected Impact: Ensure accurate biometric records for growing children

6. Interstate Data Coordination [HIGH PRIORITY]
   Enable real-time data sharing between states for seamless service
   Expected Impact: Better serve migrant populations across state boundaries

7. Enhance Self-Service Capabilities [MEDIUM PRIORITY]
   Improve mAadhaar app for remote updates and appointment booking
   Expected Impact: Reduce physical center load, especially in high-migration areas
